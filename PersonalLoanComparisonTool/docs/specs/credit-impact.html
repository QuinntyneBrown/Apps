Recommendations to Maximize Your Score

üí≥
Keep Old Credit Cards Open
Don't close your paid-off credit cards. Keeping them open with $0 balance maintains your available credit, keeps your utilization low, and preserves your credit history length.
üìÖ
Set Up Auto-Pay
Never miss a payment on your new loan. Even one late payment can drop your score by 100+ points. Auto-pay ensures perfect payment history and may get you a 0.25% rate discount.
üö´
Avoid New Credit Applications
Wait 6-12 months before applying for new credit. Multiple inquiries can compound the score impact. Let your score recover fully first.
üìä
Monitor Your Credit Monthly
Use free credit monitoring services to track your recovery progress. Watch for errors on your report and dispute them immediately. Most see improvement within 3-4 months.
üí∞
Use Cards Occasionally
Make small purchases on your credit cards and pay them off monthly. This shows active, responsible use and maintains the accounts. Just keep utilization under 10%.
