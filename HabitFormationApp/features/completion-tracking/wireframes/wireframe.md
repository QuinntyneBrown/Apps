## UI/UX Notes

### Visual Indicators
- Green checkmark: Completed on time
- Orange clock: Late completion
- Empty circle: Pending
- Red X: Missed
- Blue border: Today
- Gray dash: Not scheduled

### Animations
- Checkmark fill animation on completion
- Confetti for streak milestones
- Smooth calendar date highlighting
- Toast slide-in from bottom
- Progress bar fill animation

### Interactions
- One-tap complete from list
- Swipe right to quick complete (mobile)
- Long-press for details form (mobile)
- Click calendar date for details
- Hover calendar for tooltip
- 5-second undo window

### States
- Completed (with time and notes preview)
- Pending (with scheduled time)
- Late (with delay indicator)
- Missed (for overdue habits)

### Accessibility
- Screen reader announces completions
- Keyboard shortcuts for quick complete
- Focus indicators on all buttons
- High contrast indicators
- Time zone clearly indicated
