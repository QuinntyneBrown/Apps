# Frontend Requirements - Net Worth Tracking

## Pages
- Dashboard (`/dashboard`): Net worth overview, trends, breakdown
- Net Worth History (`/net-worth/history`): Historical charts, milestones

## Components
- NetWorthCard: Large display of current net worth with change %
- NetWorthTrendChart: Line chart showing net worth over time
- AssetLiabilityBreakdown: Stacked bar chart or pie chart
- MilestoneProgress: Progress toward next milestone
