# Community Engagement - Frontend Requirements

## Pages/Views

### 1. Show Calendar
**Route**: `/shows`
- Upcoming shows list
- Show details and registration
- Past attendance history
- Award trophy case
- Show photos gallery

### 2. Club Directory
**Route**: `/clubs`
- Club membership list
- Club benefits display
- Event calendar
- Member directory
- Renewal tracking

### 3. Share Story
**Route**: `/projects/{id}/share`
- Story editor with photos
- Platform selector (social media)
- Privacy settings
- Engagement metrics
- Community feedback

## Components
- ShowCalendar with RSVP
- AwardTrophyCase gallery
- ClubCard with benefits
- SocialShareDialog
- EngagementMetrics dashboard
