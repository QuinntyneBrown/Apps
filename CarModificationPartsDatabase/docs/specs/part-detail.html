Description

The AEM 21-478C Cold Air Intake System is designed to increase horsepower and torque by reducing air intake restriction and increasing airflow to the engine. This system features a washable Dryflow synthetic air filter that provides excellent filtration and high airflow. The intake tube is constructed from mandrel-bent aluminum for smooth airflow and includes a heat shield to isolate the filter from engine heat, ensuring cooler, denser air reaches the engine.
