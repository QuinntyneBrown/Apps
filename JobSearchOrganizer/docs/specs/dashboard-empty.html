Pro Tips for Job Search Success

  • Add applications immediately after submitting to maintain accurate tracking
  • Set reminders for follow-ups 1-2 weeks after applying
  • Prepare interview notes and research companies in advance
  • Update application status regularly to keep your pipeline current
  • Use tags and categories to organize applications by industry or priority
  • Track all offers with detailed compensation breakdowns for easy comparison
  • Leverage your network by requesting referrals for priority applications
  • Review analytics weekly to identify patterns and optimize your strategy
