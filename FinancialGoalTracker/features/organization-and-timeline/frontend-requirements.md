# Frontend Requirements - Organization and Timeline

## Pages/Views
### 1. Category View (`/goals/categories`)
- CategoryCards with aggregate progress
- GoalsByCategory lists

### 2. Timeline View (`/goals/timeline`)
- TimelineVisualization (Gantt-style)
- DeadlineAlerts panel

## UI Components
- CategoryCard: Shows total saved/target across category
- TimelineBar: Visual timeline with milestones and deadlines
- DeadlineAlert: Urgency indicator for approaching/missed deadlines
