Understanding BP Categories
Blood pressure categories are based on American Heart Association (AHA) guidelines. Your BP is determined by the highest category that either your systolic OR diastolic falls into. Consistent monitoring and staying within optimal/normal ranges reduces cardiovascular risk significantly.
