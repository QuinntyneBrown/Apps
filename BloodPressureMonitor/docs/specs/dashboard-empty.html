Getting Started Guide

1

Record Your First Reading

Take a blood pressure measurement and enter the values. Include your pulse rate and note the context (resting, after exercise, etc.) for more accurate tracking.

2

Set Your Health Goals

Define your target blood pressure range based on your doctor's recommendations. Set reminders for consistent twice-daily measurements.

3

Track Lifestyle Factors

Log medications, exercise, sodium intake, and stress levels to understand how lifestyle choices affect your blood pressure.

4

Monitor Your Progress

Review trends, analyze patterns, and generate reports for your healthcare provider. Celebrate milestones and stay motivated on your health journey.
