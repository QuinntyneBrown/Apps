# Seasonal Analysis - Frontend Requirements

## Pages

### Seasonal Dashboard (`/vehicles/{id}/seasonal`)
- **Current Season**: MPG average for current season
- **Seasonal Comparison Chart**: MPG by season over years
- **Weather Impact Tracker**: How weather affects your MPG
- **Fuel Blend Analysis**: E10 vs E15 vs E85 comparison
- **Expectation Setter**: "Normal" ranges by season

### Weather Impact Log
- Log weather conditions with fill-ups
- Visual correlation between weather and MPG
- Temperature range analysis
- Precipitation impact assessment

## UI Components

### SeasonalComparisonChart
- Four-season breakdown
- Multi-year overlay
- Contributing factors annotations
- Expected vs actual bands

### WeatherImpactWidget
- Current weather display
- Expected MPG impact
- Driving adjustment suggestions
- Historical weather-MPG correlation
