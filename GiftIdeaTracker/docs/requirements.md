# Requirements - Gift Idea Tracker

## Overview
Gift Idea Tracker helps users manage gift-giving for recipients, track occasions, capture gift ideas, coordinate with family, and maintain a history of successful gifts.

## Features & Requirements

### 1. Recipient Management
**Backend**: Store recipient profiles with personal info, preferences, sizes, important dates
**Frontend**: Recipient directory, profile editor, preference tracker, size reference

### 2. Occasion Management
**Backend**: Manage occasions (birthdays, anniversaries, holidays), recurring generation, reminder scheduling
**Frontend**: Occasion calendar, reminder settings, past occasion history

### 3. Gift Ideas
**Backend**: Store gift ideas with descriptions, categories, price ranges, priority, sources
**Frontend**: Idea bank, search/filter, sharing interface, claiming system

### 4. Purchase Management
**Backend**: Track purchases (date, cost, store, delivery status), wrapping status, gift given status
**Frontend**: Shopping list, delivery tracker, wrapping checklist, gift history

### 5. Budget Management
**Backend**: Set budgets per occasion/recipient, track spending, calculate annual totals, threshold alerts
**Frontend**: Budget dashboard, spending visualization, overage warnings, annual summary

### 6. Wishlist Monitoring
**Backend**: Track recipient wishlists, monitor price changes, sync with Amazon/registries
**Frontend**: Wishlist browser, price drop alerts, quick-add from wishlist

### 7. Coordination
**Backend**: Manage gift coordination groups, track contributions, pool funds for group gifts
**Frontend**: Coordination dashboard, contribution tracker, group gift planner

### 8. Reminders
**Backend**: Schedule shopping reminders, trigger last-minute alerts (1-3 days before)
**Frontend**: Reminder notifications, shopping window calendar, urgent alerts

### 9. History & Analytics
**Backend**: Store gift history per recipient, analyze success patterns, identify favorites
**Frontend**: Gift history timeline, success rate dashboard, recommendation engine

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Mobile-responsive for on-the-go shopping
- Photo storage for gift ideas and receipts
- Real-time price tracking for wishlists
- Secure storage of financial data
- Export gift history and budgets
