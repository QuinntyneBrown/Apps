# Spot Management - Frontend Requirements

## Pages/Views

### 1. Spot Map
- Interactive map with spot markers
- Filter by rating/type
- Search nearby spots
- Add new spot with GPS
- Cluster markers for density
- Satellite/terrain toggle

### 2. Spot Details
- Photos and description
- Depth and structure info
- Average rating and reviews
- Recent catches at spot
- Seasonal success patterns
- Directions and access info

### 3. My Spots
- List of saved spots
- Private spot management
- Sharing controls
- Spot success analytics
- Export spot data

## Components
- InteractiveMap with spot markers
- SpotMarker with rating badge
- SpotInfoCard
- RatingStars
- SeasonalSuccessChart
- ShareDialog with privacy settings
