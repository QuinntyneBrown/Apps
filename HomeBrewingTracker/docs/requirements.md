# Home Brewing Tracker - Requirements

## Executive Summary
Track beer brewing from recipe to bottle, monitor fermentation, log tasting notes, and perfect recipes through detailed brewing analytics.

## Core Features

### 1. Recipe Management
- Create and edit brewing recipes
- Clone recipes for experimentation
- Scale recipes for different batch sizes
- Ingredient database and calculator
- Recipe sharing with community

### 2. Brew Day Tracking
- Schedule brew sessions
- Record mash temperatures
- Log gravity readings (OG/FG)
- Track brewing timeline
- Note issues and adjustments

### 3. Fermentation Monitoring
- Temperature logging
- Fermentation activity tracking
- Gravity check scheduling
- Secondary fermentation tracking
- Completion detection

### 4. Packaging
- Bottling session tracking
- Kegging management
- Carbonation monitoring
- Label design and printing
- Inventory management

### 5. Tasting & Evaluation
- Formal tasting notes
- Score batches (appearance, aroma, flavor, mouthfeel)
- Compare batches
- Rate success
- Improvement identification

### 6. Inventory & Shopping
- Ingredient inventory
- Freshness tracking
- Shopping list generation
- Supplier management
- Cost tracking

## Technical Requirements
- Temperature sensor integration
- Recipe import/export (BeerXML)
- Batch photo upload
- Competition entry tracking
