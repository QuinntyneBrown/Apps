About This Book

The Manager's Path is a comprehensive guide for tech leaders navigating the journey from individual contributor to senior leadership positions. Camille Fournier draws from her extensive experience in engineering management to provide practical advice on the challenges and decisions that come with leadership roles in technology companies.

The book covers essential topics including mentoring, tech lead responsibilities, managing teams, managing managers, and executive leadership. Each chapter builds on the previous one, creating a roadmap for growing as a technical leader. The book emphasizes both the technical and human aspects of management, offering real-world examples and actionable strategies.
