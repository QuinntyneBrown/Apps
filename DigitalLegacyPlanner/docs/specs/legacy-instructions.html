Personal Letter

To my dear family and friends,

If you're reading this, it means my time has come. I want you to know that I've prepared this digital legacy plan to make things easier for you during what I know will be a difficult time.

First and foremost, please don't feel overwhelmed by the task ahead. I've organized everything to be as straightforward as possible. Michael, as the primary executor, you have access to all the information you'll need. Emily and Jennifer are here to help you as well.

My most precious digital possessions are the photos and videos we've shared over the years. They're stored in my iCloud account and Google Photos. Please download them and share them with everyone in the family. These memories are my greatest treasure, and I want them preserved.

For my social media accounts, I'd like you to download any photos or posts that seem meaningful, then close the accounts. I've left specific instructions for each platform. Please don't feel you need to maintain them - I'd prefer a clean goodbye rather than abandoned profiles.

The financial accounts are documented with all necessary information. Work with our family attorney and financial advisor to handle these properly. Everything is organized and up to date.

Thank you for taking care of all of this. I know it's not easy, but I hope this planning makes it at least a little bit easier. Remember the good times, share the stories, and keep our memories alive in your hearts.

I love you all so very much.

With all my love,
Sarah
