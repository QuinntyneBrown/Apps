Emergency Access Not Configured

Access Triggers

Triggers are events that activate the emergency access protocol. Choose which conditions should grant your beneficiaries access to your digital legacy.

Death Certificate Verification
Beneficiaries submit an official death certificate for verification. Most secure and definitive method.
Inactivity Period
No account activity for 180 days triggers the waiting period. You'll receive notifications to prevent accidental activation.
Medical Incapacitation
Medical documentation of incapacitation verified by designated healthcare contacts.
Manual Beneficiary Request
Beneficiaries can request access, triggering a waiting period and multiple verification attempts to reach you.

Waiting Period

The waiting period is a safety measure. After a trigger event, beneficiaries must wait this amount of time before gaining access. During this period, you'll receive notifications and can cancel the process if it was activated accidentally.
