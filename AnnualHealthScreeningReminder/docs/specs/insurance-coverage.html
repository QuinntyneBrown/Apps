Age & Risk Factor Guidelines
Coverage frequency is based on recommended guidelines for your age and risk factors. Some screenings require pre-authorization.
Preventive vs. Diagnostic
If a screening leads to additional diagnostic procedures or treatment, those services may be subject to your regular copay and deductible.
