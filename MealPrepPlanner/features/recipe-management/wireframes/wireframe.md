### Multi-Step Form Sections
1. **Basic Info**: Name, description, category, cuisine, difficulty
2. **Timing**: Prep time, cook time (auto-calculated total)
3. **Ingredients**: Add ingredients with quantities and units
4. **Instructions**: Step-by-step directions
5. **Tags & Image**: Searchable tags, recipe photo upload
6. **Nutrition**: Auto-calculate or manual entry
