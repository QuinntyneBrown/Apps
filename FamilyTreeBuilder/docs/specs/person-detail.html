Michael is a passionate architect who has designed several notable buildings in Seattle. He graduated from MIT with honors in 1994 and immediately started working for a prestigious architecture firm. After gaining 10 years of experience, he founded his own practice in 2004.

Outside of work, Michael is an avid sailor and photographer. He loves spending weekends on his sailboat exploring the Puget Sound. His photography has been featured in several local exhibitions, primarily focusing on architectural and nautical subjects.

Michael is known in the family for organizing annual reunions and maintaining strong connections with relatives across the country.
