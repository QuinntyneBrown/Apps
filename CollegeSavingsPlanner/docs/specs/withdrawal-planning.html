üìã Withdrawal Guidelines

‚úì Qualified Expenses

Tax-free withdrawals are allowed for tuition, fees, books, supplies, equipment required for enrollment, and room & board (if at least half-time student).

‚ö† Keep Documentation

Save receipts and billing statements for all qualified expenses. You may need them for tax purposes or IRS audits.

üí° Timing Matters

Withdrawals must be made in the same year the expenses are paid. Don't withdraw in December for January tuition.

üìä Coordinate with Credits

You cannot claim the same expenses for both 529 withdrawals and education tax credits (American Opportunity Credit).

üìÑ Tax Reporting

  • Qualified withdrawals are tax-free
  • 1099-Q form issued for all withdrawals
  • Report on Form 1040 if requested
  • Non-qualified withdrawals face 10% penalty plus income tax on earnings
