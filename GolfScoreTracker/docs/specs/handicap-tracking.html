How Your Handicap is Calculated
Your Handicap Index is calculated using the best 8 of your most recent 20 Score Differentials. A Score Differential measures the difficulty of a round by comparing your adjusted gross score to the course rating and slope. The system updates automatically after each posted round. Lower differentials indicate better performance relative to par.
