# Photography Session Logger - Requirements

## Overview
Professional photography workflow management system for planning sessions, tracking equipment usage, managing photo projects, analyzing performance, and growing photography business.

## Target Users
- Professional photographers
- Photography enthusiasts
- Studio owners
- Event photographers
- Portrait and landscape photographers
- Photography students

## Core Features

### 1. Session Management
- Schedule photo sessions
- Track session details and settings
- Log real-time shooting data
- Document session outcomes
- Manage client sessions
- Session analytics

### 2. Photo Project Management
- Create thematic projects
- Track project milestones
- Curate photo selections
- Organize deliverables
- Portfolio management
- Project completion tracking

### 3. Equipment Management
- Camera body inventory
- Lens catalog and usage
- Equipment purchases
- Maintenance scheduling
- Failure tracking
- ROI calculations

### 4. Photo Organization
- Import and catalog photos
- Culling and selection workflow
- Photo editing tracking
- Rating and tagging system
- Metadata management
- Version control

### 5. Client & Business
- Client session booking
- Photo delivery tracking
- Client feedback collection
- Testimonial management
- Revenue tracking
- Contract management

### 6. Financial Tracking
- Session revenue logging
- Photo sales tracking
- Expense management
- Tax preparation support
- Profitability analysis
- Pricing optimization

### 7. Skill Development
- Technique experimentation
- Skill mastery tracking
- Inspirational work studies
- Education completion
- Style evolution
- Creative growth

### 8. Exhibition & Sharing
- Social media sharing
- Contest submissions
- Competition results
- Exhibition participation
- Community engagement
- Portfolio presentation

## Technical Requirements
- .NET 8 backend, SQL Server
- Large file handling for RAW photos
- EXIF data extraction
- Cloud storage integration
- Photo editing software integration
- Mobile app for on-location logging

## Success Metrics
- Session completion rate > 95%
- Client satisfaction > 4.8/5
- Equipment usage tracking > 90%
- Revenue per session growth
- Portfolio update frequency
