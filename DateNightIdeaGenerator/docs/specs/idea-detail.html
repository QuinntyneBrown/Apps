üìã Planning Guide

Make this date extra special with thoughtful planning. Here's everything you need to know to pull off the perfect sunset picnic.

What to Bring

  • Large picnic blanket or outdoor mat
  • Insulated cooler or picnic basket
  • Portable speaker for background music
  • Candles or string lights for ambiance (battery-operated)
  • Reusable plates, utensils, and cups
  • Napkins and wet wipes for easy cleanup
  • Trash bag for leaving no trace
  • Light jacket or blanket for when it gets cooler

Food & Drink Ideas

  • Assorted cheeses and crackers
  • Fresh fruits (strawberries, grapes, watermelon)
  • Charcuterie meats or vegetarian alternatives
  • Sandwiches or wraps (easy to eat outdoors)
  • Hummus and vegetables
  • Sparkling water, wine, or your favorite beverages
  • Chocolate-covered strawberries for dessert

üí° Pro Tips

  • Timing is everything: Check the sunset time for your area and arrive 30-45 minutes early to set up and enjoy the golden hour light.
  • Location scouting: Visit the park beforehand to find the perfect spot with a good view and not too much foot traffic.
  • Weather check: Always check the forecast and have a backup indoor picnic plan if needed.
  • Surprise element: Hide a small gift, handwritten note, or a favorite treat in the basket as a surprise.
  • Photo opportunity: Bring a camera or tripod to capture sunset photos together.
  • Entertainment: Pack a card game, conversation starters, or a book to read together.

üìç Recommended Locations

Riverside Park

Why it's great: Stunning river views, plenty of open space, and designated picnic areas. The sunset reflects beautifully on the water.

Address: 123 Riverside Drive

Parking: Free parking available, but can get busy on weekends

Hilltop Gardens

Why it's great: Elevated views of the city skyline, well-maintained gardens, and less crowded than popular parks.

Address: 456 Summit Avenue

Parking: Limited street parking, arrive early

Lakeside Commons

Why it's great: Beautiful lake setting, walking trails nearby, and often has live music on weekends.

Address: 789 Lakeshore Boulevard

Parking: Paid parking lot ($5)
