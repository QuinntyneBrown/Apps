# Frontend Requirements - Environment Tracking

## Components

### EnvironmentLogForm
- Temperature input (F/C)
- Noise level slider (0-100 dB)
- Light level slider (0-100 lux)
- Humidity percentage
- Auto-fill from smart devices

### OptimalConditionsDisplay
- Your optimal temperature range
- Your optimal noise level
- Your optimal light level
- Comparison to general recommendations

### EnvironmentAlerts
- Real-time alerts for suboptimal conditions
- Suggestions for corrections
- Smart device control integration

### EnvironmentCorrelationChart
- Chart showing environment vs sleep quality
- Heatmap of temperature vs quality
- Optimal zones highlighted

## Pages

### Environment Dashboard (/environment)
- Current conditions
- Optimal conditions display
- Recent environment logs
- Smart device connections

## Interactions
- Log environment manually or auto-sync
- View optimal conditions
- Adjust bedroom based on recommendations
- Connect smart home devices
