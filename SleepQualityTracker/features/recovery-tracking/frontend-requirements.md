# Frontend Requirements - Recovery Tracking

## Components

### RecoveryScoreCard
- Large recovery score (0-100)
- Color-coded readiness indicator
- HRV and resting HR display
- Training readiness recommendation

### RecoveryTrendChart
- Line chart of recovery over time
- Color zones for readiness levels
- Correlation with training/activity

### TrainingRecommendations
- Based on recovery status
- Suggested intensity levels
- Rest day recommendations

## Pages

### Recovery Dashboard (/recovery)
- Today's recovery score
- Trend chart
- Training recommendations
- HRV and HR metrics

## Interactions
- View daily recovery immediately after wake
- Track recovery trends
- Adjust training based on recommendations
