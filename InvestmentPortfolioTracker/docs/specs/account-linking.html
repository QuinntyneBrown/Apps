Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is account linking?

We use bank-level security with 256-bit encryption. Your credentials are never stored on our servers. We only have read-only access to view your holdings and cannot execute trades or transfers.

How often do accounts sync?

By default, accounts sync every 4 hours automatically. You can also manually sync at any time. Some brokerages may have limitations on sync frequency.

What if my broker isn't listed?

You can manually add holdings or request support for your broker. We're constantly adding new integrations. You can also export data from your broker and import it here.

Can I disconnect an account?

Yes, you can disconnect any account at any time. Your historical data will be preserved, but new syncs will stop. You can also revoke access directly from your brokerage's settings.
