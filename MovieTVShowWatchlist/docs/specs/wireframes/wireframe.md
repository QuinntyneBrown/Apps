## Key Interactions

### Add to Watchlist Flow
1. User clicks "+ Add Content" or searches
2. Search results display with posters
3. Click on content opens detail modal
4. Click "Add to Watchlist" button
5. Priority selector appears (High/Medium/Low)
6. Optional: Add mood category, notes
7. Content added with confirmation message

### Mark as Watched Flow
1. User finds content (watchlist, search, history)
2. Click "Mark as Watched" or âœ“ icon
3. Modal opens with:
   - Watch date picker
   - Platform selector
   - Rating (optional)
   - Companions (optional)
4. Submit adds to history
5. Removes from watchlist
6. Updates statistics

### Rating and Review Flow
1. From content detail or history
2. Click star rating (1-5 stars)
3. Optional: Click "Write Review"
4. Text area expands
5. Add spoiler warning checkbox
6. Submit saves rating/review
7. Appears in ratings & reviews page

## Design System

### Colors
- Primary: Deep Blue (#1a237e)
- Secondary: Teal (#00897b)
- Accent: Orange (#ff6f00)
- Success: Green (#43a047)
- Warning: Amber (#ffa000)
- Error: Red (#e53935)
- Background: Light Gray (#f5f5f5)
- Card: White (#ffffff)

### Typography
- Headers: Roboto Bold
- Body: Roboto Regular
- Captions: Roboto Light

### Icons
- Material Design Icons
- Streaming platform logos
- Star ratings
- Priority indicators (flame, flag)

### Spacing
- Grid: 8px base unit
- Card padding: 16px
- Section margin: 24px
- Component gap: 12px
