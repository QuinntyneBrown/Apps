Getting Started Tips
1
Start with Daily Notes
Create a daily note to journal your thoughts and ideas. It's a great way to build the habit.
2
Link Your Notes
Use [[double brackets]] to link to other notes. This creates powerful connections in your knowledge base.
3
Use Tags Wisely
Organize your notes with tags like #project, #idea, or #book-notes for easy filtering and discovery.
4
Process Your Inbox
Use the inbox to quickly capture ideas on the go, then process them into proper notes later.
